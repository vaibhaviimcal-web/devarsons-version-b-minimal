01 — Legacy

102 Years of Excellence

From a trading company in 1923 to a global innovation leader in specialty colorants

Devarsons is an ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and OHSAS 18001 accredited company developing and producing specialty colorants. With over 50 years of accumulated experience in color application, our product range is specifically designed to cater to customers in cosmetics, crop protection, plastics, petroleum, and more.

We don't produce any one class of dye—we create an assortment of dyes with different chemistries to suit highly evolved applications. Our dyes comply with worldwide regulations, and our facilities are designed to eliminate foreign contamination.
